This Week’s Mississippi Moments

This week, we’re highlighting three powerful Mississippi stories—a daring undercover mission to dismantle a hate group, a community’s urgent search for a missing teen, and an ongoing fight for justice after a tragic triple homicide.

01

The Man Who Infiltrated the KKK

Jones County, Mississippi is known for standing up for what’s right. In the 1960s, that legacy continued with Tom Landrum, a local farmer and juvenile court counselor who bravely went undercover for the FBI to take down the KKK—a dangerous mission that put his family at risk and revealed shocking truths.

Read More
02

Disappearance in Waynesboro

19-year-old John Quavontae Gandy vanished on January 8, 2025, after leaving his girlfriend’s apartment in Waynesboro, Mississippi. Witnesses saw him approach a red or burgundy vehicle before he disappeared. Details remain unclear, and authorities need your help. Even the smallest tip could be the key to finding John.

Read More
03

Triple Homicide Shakes State Line, Mississippi

48 days have passed since the New Year’s Day triple homicide at Clark’s gas station in State Line, MS, leaving Rhonda Denson, Lester Campbell, and Jason Thornton dead. The shooter remains unidentified, despite surveillance footage and a $100,000 reward. Justice is still sought—any tip could make a difference.

MAGNOLIA PICKS

BRICK & SPOON - GULFPORT, MS

Brick & Spoon offers a distinctive dining experience, blending Southern comfort food with creative twists.

Build Your Own Bloody Mary: Choose from over 50 options—select your vodka, adjust the spice, and add toppings like veggies, herbs, meats, and cheeses.

Bananas in Pajamas: Crispy banana eggrolls served with a rich Foster sauce.

Café Beignets: Classic New Orleans-style beignets with powdered sugar and marmalade.

Bayou Benedict: A regional twist on the classic, featuring bayou shrimp and house-made hollandaise.

Bacon Cali Burger: Pecan-smoked bacon, avocado, pepper jack, and house aioli atop a homemade patty.

Experience Southern comfort food with a modern twist—perfect for breakfast, brunch, or lunch.
